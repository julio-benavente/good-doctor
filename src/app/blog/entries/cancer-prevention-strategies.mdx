## FAQ

### How often should I undergo cancer screenings?

The frequency of cancer screenings depends on various factors, including your age, gender, family history, and personal health. It's crucial to consult with your healthcare provider to determine a screening schedule tailored to your individual risk factors and needs.

### Is genetic counseling necessary for everyone?

While not everyone may need genetic counseling, individuals with a family history of certain cancers or those with identified genetic risk factors may benefit from genetic counseling. It helps assess the risk of hereditary conditions and provides valuable information for personalized prevention strategies.
